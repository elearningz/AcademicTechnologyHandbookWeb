Instructor Support

Instructor Support is offered by the IT Office and can be requested by emailing it.nursing@utoronto.ca or contacting members of the team directly.

While this handbook is a good place to start in terms of orienting yourself with the teaching technology available we definitely recommend reaching out for some consulation if you have any questions or ideas about implementing existing or new technology into your teaching practice.
